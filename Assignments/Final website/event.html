Cycle The Continental Divide!

This is our biggest event of the year! Please come join us in our cycle of fun.
Registration Deadline: July 10th
Dates: July 20-25th, five day riding extravaganza!
Route: 150 miles, Continental Divide National Park Scenic Trail.
Costs: $100 per person (includes camping gear, food and beverages), $125 if rentals are needed
Requirements: Bike, helmet, mountain biking knowledge/ experience, must be over 18 years old, if no bike rentals may be purchased for the ride for an additional charge.
