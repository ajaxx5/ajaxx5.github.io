Join us on one of our Adventures!

Snail River Trail

Our first tour for beginning mountain bikers is the Snail River Trail. This trail is a great starting point for those who just want to get used to riding int he mountains.

Beginner Guided Tours: Monday-Saturday 9am-4pm
Price: $20 per person, group specials starting at $100

Snakeridge Trail

The second tour we have is for intermediate-advanced mountain bikers. Our second tour is on the Snakeridge trail, this trail is much more advanced but can still be fun and relaxing depending on the speed you want to take.

Intermediate-Advanced Guided Tours: Monday-Saturday 9am-4pm
Price: $25 per person, group specials starting at $100
